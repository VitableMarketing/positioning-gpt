### Summary of Meeting between ComForCare Dallas Park Cities and Vitable Health

**Participants:**
- Ryan Eiker (Vitable Health)
- Laura Smithen (ComForCare Dallas Park Cities)

**Main Topics Discussed:**
1. **Current Benefits Offering:**
   - ComForCare initially went with a plan from Life Exec, but it did not meet their expectations due to limited availability of in-network doctors.
   - Many employees are hourly workers without much discretionary income, and they need a cost-effective healthcare option.
   - Laura expressed that the current offerings are not providing enough tangible benefits for employees, which makes even a small premium feel like a wasted expense.

2. **Motivation for Offering New Benefits:**
   - ComForCare is looking for a plan that provides real value to employees without breaking their budget, considering their financial limitations.
   - The focus is on finding a plan that would offer practical and affordable healthcare solutions for hourly workers.

3. **Interest in Vitable Health’s Offerings:**
   - Ryan explained Vitable Health's approach, which is tailored to provide value by ensuring that hourly workers get the healthcare they need at an affordable cost.
   - Vitable’s plan focuses on primary care, sick visits, and essential medical needs without the burden of high costs or complicated reimbursement processes.

4. **Next Steps:**
   - Laura showed interest in seeing details about Vitable’s plans and whether they could align with ComForCare’s needs.
   - A follow-up meeting was discussed to explore plan options further and see if Vitable’s offerings would provide a better solution for ComForCare’s employees.
