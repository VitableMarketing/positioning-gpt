**Main Topics Discussed:**
1. **Current Benefits Offering:**

2. **Motivation for Considering New Benefits:**
   - Jodi mentioned that Family Ties is always exploring better options for their staff, looking for something more cost-effective that provides greater value.

3. **Interest in Vitable Health’s Offerings:**
   - Lauren discussed Vitable’s affordable healthcare options, which are specifically tailored to meet the needs of hourly and entry-level workers.
   - The discussion focused on understanding if Vitable’s offerings could serve as a more beneficial and cost-effective alternative to the existing MEC plan.

4. **Next Steps:**

   - A follow-up was suggested to provide more detailed comparisons and evaluate whether Vitable’s options would be suitable for Family Ties.
