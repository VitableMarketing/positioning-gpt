### Summary of Meeting between Home Instead and Vitable Health

**Participants:**
- Lauren Brady (Vitable Health)
- Mike Neupauer (Home Instead)

**Main Topics Discussed:**

1. **TB Testing Challenges and Options:**
   - Home Instead currently utilizes MedExpress and local urgent care centers for TB testing.
   - Mike expressed that the current two-step PPD process is inconvenient for their staff due to the multiple required visits and the limited availability of testing sites.
   - Cost concerns were raised regarding TB testing, which currently costs between $70 to $75 for both steps.
   - Lauren introduced Vitable's Quantiferon Gold test as a more efficient one-step TB testing solution that includes lab partnerships with Quest and Labcorp.
   - Discussion on the pricing model for TB testing, including the potential for bulk billing and bundling of tests on an annual or quarterly basis.

2. **Employee Benefits Offerings:**
   - Home Instead currently offers a MEC (Minimum Essential Coverage) plan and noted low participation from employees.
   - Most employees are either covered by Medicaid, Medicare, or their spouse's health insurance, with only a few employees enrolled in Home Instead's health coverage.
   - Lauren presented Vitable’s healthcare benefits, focusing on how they could align with ACA requirements and better serve employees' needs.
   - The conversation also touched upon 401(k) options, though Mike indicated a low interest level among part-time employees for such programs.

3. **Next Steps and Considerations:**
   - Mike plans to discuss the new TB testing options with his recruiter, Amy, and assess whether Vitable’s testing solution fits their needs.
   - Lauren will provide additional information about the TB testing pricing and available locations to help Home Instead make an informed decision.
   - A potential follow-up call to further evaluate health benefits was discussed.

### Company Information:

1. **Company Name:** Home Instead
2. **Company Industry:** Home Care Services
3. **Company Size:** Approximately 80 employees, mainly part-time
4. **Previous Knowledge of the Affordable Care Act:** Familiar with ACA requirements, actively monitoring FTE counts to ensure compliance.
5. **Core Challenges:**
   - The current TB testing process is burdensome and limits onboarding efficiency.
   - Low participation in current employee benefits offerings due to coverage through other sources.
6. **What They're Looking For:**
   - A simplified, efficient TB testing solution that eases the onboarding process.
   - Affordable healthcare benefits that align with compliance requirements and attract more full-time employees.

### Next Steps Suggested:
- Vitable Health to provide follow-up information about pricing and testing locations.
- Home Instead to consider the feedback from their recruiting team and evaluate whether to proceed with a trial of Vitable's testing services.
